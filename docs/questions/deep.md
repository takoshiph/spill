# Deep — Question Bank

Slow, vulnerable, meaningful. Answered out loud, about yourself. Cards are grouped by **depth tier** (the deck deals Warm-up → Open up → Deep). Theme tags in `[brackets]` are for balancing only — players never see them. Edit freely.

---

## Warm-up

1. What's something small that made you genuinely happy this week? `[joy]`
2. Where did you grow up, and what's one thing about it that shaped you? `[roots]`
3. What's a dream you had as a kid — and do you ever still think about it? `[dreams]`
4. Who in your life right now makes you feel most like yourself? `[love]`
5. What's something you're proud of that you don't get to talk about much? `[joy]`
6. What did a "good life" look like to you five years ago? `[values]`
7. What's a tiny ritual or habit that keeps you grounded? `[values]`
8. What's something you're looking forward to in the next year? `[dreams]`

## Open up

9. What's a belief you held strongly five years ago that you've let go of? `[values]`
10. When did you last feel truly seen by someone? What did they do? `[love]`
11. What's a goal you're chasing right now that actually scares you? `[dreams]`
12. What's something you needed to hear as a kid but never did? `[roots]`
13. What's a problem you're quietly working through these days? `[struggles]`
14. What does feeling loved actually look like for you — specifically? `[love]`
15. What part of yourself do you hide from most people in this room? `[struggles]`
16. What's a lesson from your family you've kept — and one you've dropped? `[roots]`
17. What do you wish you were braver about? `[struggles]`
18. When do you feel most like the person you want to be? `[joy]`

## Deep

19. What's a fear you've never said out loud? `[struggles]`
20. What's something you're still trying to forgive yourself for? `[struggles]`
21. Who do you miss right now, and what would you say to them? `[love]`
22. What's a dream you quietly gave up on — and do you want it back? `[dreams]`
23. What are you most afraid people will find out about you? `[struggles]`
24. When have you felt the loneliest, even surrounded by people? `[struggles]`
25. What's a relationship in your life you wish you could repair? `[love]`
26. What's the version of your future that genuinely excites you — say it out loud? `[dreams]`
27. What's something true about you that you've never told anyone here? `[values]`
28. If you knew you couldn't fail, what would you do with the next ten years? `[dreams]`
